Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Veva

Veva is an online grocery platform that allows you to shop for daily essentials and groceries at your convenience.

After logging in, go to your User Account → Order Tracking tab to see the current status of your order.

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

You can cancel your order from your account before it is marked as “Out for Delivery.” Once shipped, it cannot be cancelled.
