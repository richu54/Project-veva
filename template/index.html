About Us

Welcome to Veva - The Complete Grocery, your trusted online destination for all your grocery needs, delivered fresh to your doorstep across India.

Our journey began on January 1st, 2015, in the vibrant city of Ernakulam, Kerala. From our humble beginnings, we envisioned a seamless and convenient way for everyone to access high-quality groceries, saving them time and effort.

At Veva, we believe that grocery shopping should be a hassle-free experience. That's why we've curated a comprehensive selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, household essentials, and more, all available at your fingertips. We are committed to sourcing the best quality products, ensuring freshness and value for your money.

More than just groceries, we deliver convenience. We understand the demands of modern life, and our efficient delivery network is designed to bring your groceries to you on your preferred days. In most major cities, we offer delivery 7 days a week, with convenient time slots to fit your schedule. (Please check your specific location for delivery availability and schedule.)
